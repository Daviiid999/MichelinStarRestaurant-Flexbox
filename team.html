The Chef

Álvaro Arriaga, renowned chef and soul of the Arriaga Restaurant, is the engine that drives creativity and innovation in every dish that comes out of our kitchen. His passion for flavors and focus on seasonal ingredients and the highest quality and freshness lead us to create culinary experiences that equally surprise and delight all those who try his cuisine.

Our Kitchen

Our passion for excellence is not only reflected in every dish we serve, but also in our constant search for exceptional talent. We believe that the success of our signature cuisine and our gastronomic experience lies in the talent and dedication of our team. That's why we are always looking for creative, passionate and committed minds to join us on this exciting culinary journey.

In our desire to innovate and improve ourselves day after day, we look for people who share our vision and are willing to make a difference in the world of haute cuisine. We value diversity and originality, as we know that each member of our team brings their own special touch that enriches our work and allows us to constantly surprise our guests.

We are proud to have a diverse and united team, where each member brings their own essence and personality, but we all share the same goal: to make your experience at the Arriaga Restaurant simply unforgettable. We are here to pamper you, to surprise you and to provide you with excellent service in a welcoming and warm atmosphere.

Team / Family

At the heart of the Arriaga Restaurant is an exceptional team, where passion for gastronomy meets creativity and the commitment to provide unique experiences to anyone who wants to come visit us and enjoy a unique experience in Granada. Led by the passionate and charismatic Álvaro Arriaga, our team is made up of talented professionals who share the same vision as him: to make each visit to the restaurant an unforgettable culinary adventure.

We are proud to have a diverse and united team, where each member brings their own essence and personality, but we all share the same goal: to make your experience at the Arriaga Restaurant simply unforgettable. We are here to pamper you, to surprise you and to provide you with excellent service in a welcoming and warm atmosphere.

But the success of Restaurante Arriaga is not limited to one person. Behind every exquisite dish and every careful attention there is a professional, committed and passionate team that works hard to offer the best to everyone who visits us. From our talented chefs and expert sommeliers to our friendly service staff, each member of the Arriaga team contributes their talent.
